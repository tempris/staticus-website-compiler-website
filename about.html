About Staticus Website Compiler

A Note from the Author

Staticus Website Compiler is a personal project born out of a desire to create static webpage content with greater flexibility and functionality than what existing tools offered. Unsatisfied with the available options, I set out to build a tool that would allow me to write webpages in a traditional manner while integrating features that enhance the development experience.

Over the years, this tool has evolved into something truly useful, streamlining my workflow and enabling me to produce high-quality static websites efficiently. After seeing others online express similar frustrations and a desire for a tool with these capabilities, I decided to make Staticus available to the public. My hope is that it will provide a valuable solution for developers, designers, and content creators who want to maintain control over their webpages while benefiting from modern features.

Whether you're a professional looking to simplify your workflow or an enthusiast seeking an intuitive way to build static content, I believe Staticus can be a powerful addition to your toolkit. I hope you find it as helpful as I have.

- James Steele Seeley

Inspiration

Staticus traces its early inspiration of the gulp-es6 project by Alessandro Castellani (Alecaddd) .
